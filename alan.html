Alan Prost

Alan Prost was a great driver and one of Sennas biggest rivals. He was a French driver who raced with Mclaren, Renault, Ferrari, and Williams. He had four driver championships and the most wins until Michael Schumacher. He got fired from Ferrari after publicly insulting them which was the team he was at and after two collisions with Senna.
