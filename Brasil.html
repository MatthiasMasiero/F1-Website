Brasil 2022

This is Lewis Hamiltons Brasil 2022 livery helmet. It is meant to resemble his respect to his favorite country to race in with his favorite fans. He was granted honorary Brasillian citizenship in the year 2021 and this was his first race on the Sao Paulo circuit with that title. The helmet also is a tribute to his idol driver Ayrton Senna. This helmet is priceless but you can buy it for $5200.
